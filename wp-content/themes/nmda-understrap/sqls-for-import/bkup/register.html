Section 4: Logo Classification: (select all that apply)
NEW MEXICO – Grown with Tradition®
This classification is used by farmers and ranchers who grow, raise and/or handle produce, nuts, livestock & poultry, meat products, horticultural products, as well as other crops
NEW MEXICO – Taste the Tradition®
This classification is used by manufactures of food and beverage products which are 51% processed, manufactured or made in the State of New Mexico. This includes sauces, seasonings, dried foods, animal products, beverages (alcoholic and non-alcoholic), baked goods, sweets and other foods
NEW MEXICO – Grown with Tradition®/Taste the Tradition® Associate Member
This classification is used by those whom support New Mexico agriculture and Logo Program members. These include retailers, farmers markets, CSAs and roadside stands who sell three or more Taste/Grown with Tradition products, restaurants who serve dishes that incorporate one or more New Mexico food ingredient, agritourism operations, artisan and crafted products, pet food manufacturers and organizations and associations that support New Mexico agriculture. Note: Any nonfood items must be made in the state for the benefit of New Mexico agriculture and at least 51 % agriculture origin by weight, and that agriculture base must have been grown in New Mexico, unless approved by the Logo Program administrator
